# Stories Folder

Place each story in its own folder.

Recommended structure:

```text
stories/
├── story-one/
│   ├── STORY.md
│   ├── CANON.md
│   ├── CHARACTERS.md
│   ├── LOCATIONS.md
│   ├── TIMELINE.md
│   ├── SCENES.md
│   └── assets/
├── story-two/
└── story-three/
```

Each story may share characters and environments with other stories. Use stable IDs and document crossover relationships rather than duplicating conflicting canon.

See `docs/content/STORIES_FOLDER.md` for full rules.
