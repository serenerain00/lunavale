# Luna Vault

Luna Vault is an explorable cinematic universe built around original stories, recurring characters, meaningful environments, and premium audience experiences.

The platform combines:

- A cinematic streaming library
- An interactive world map
- Environment-based story discovery
- Character and relationship exploration
- Premium memberships
- Behind-the-scenes production content
- Melissa Casole's creator and creative-technology portfolio

## The Core Idea

Visitors do not only browse episodes.

They enter locations.

The lakehouse, farmhouse, race track, park, coffee shop, downtown, garage, barn, dock, mountain roads, and future environments act as living story spaces. Each contains scenes, memories, objects, character connections, hidden details, and premium discoveries.

A journal may open Luna's private thoughts.  
A firepit may reveal a conversation with Tyson.  
A helmet may lead into Tyson's track history.  
A mug in the farmhouse kitchen may connect to Luna and Josh's past.

## Product Position

Luna Vault should feel like:

- An independent streaming service
- An explorable story world
- A cinematic visual novel
- A premium creator membership
- A professional creative portfolio

It should not feel like:

- A generic portfolio
- A link-in-bio site
- A Patreon clone
- A basic video gallery
- A literal Netflix copy
- A game filled with meaningless points

## Technical Direction

Recommended foundation:

- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Vercel
- GSAP and `@gsap/react`
- Managed authentication
- Managed relational database
- Hosted adaptive video
- Stripe
- Structured Markdown, MDX, JSON, or a headless CMS
- Privacy-conscious analytics

## MVP Success

A new visitor should be able to:

1. Understand the Luna premise quickly
2. Enter the world through a cinematic environment
3. Discover a story through exploration
4. Watch at least one public scene
5. move easily between immersive and conventional navigation
6. Understand what membership unlocks
7. Create an account or subscribe
8. Return and continue exploring
